# Methodology

## Goal
Estimate housing affordability in Toms River, NJ by measuring income remaining after major housing-related costs.

## Current workflow
1. Load parcel geometry.
2. Join parcel tax and sales attributes.
3. Clip FEMA flood hazard polygons to Toms River.
4. Export interactive local maps for inspection.

## Planned workflow
1. Add DEM elevation data.
2. Join elevation and flood context to parcels.
3. Integrate insurance-related data.
4. Build cost estimates at the parcel or tract level.
5. Develop an interactive dashboard for analysis.
